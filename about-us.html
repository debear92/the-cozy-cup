Our Story

The Cozy Cup was founded in 2015 by a group of friends who were passionate about good food and great coffee. From the beginning, our founders were committed to sourcing the freshest, highest-quality ingredients for our menu. They worked closely with local farmers and producers to ensure that everything we serve is not only delicious, but also sustainable and environmentally responsible.
Over the years, our café has become a beloved fixture in the Cork community. We’ve hosted countless events, from poetry readings to live music performances, and have welcomed people from all walks of life through our doors.
Our customers are our family, and we’re grateful for each and every one of them.

At our café, we’re committed to providing a warm and welcoming environment where everyone feels at home. We believe that food and drink are about more than just sustenance; they’re about bringing people together and fostering a sense of community. That’s why we’re committed to using sustainable and environmentally responsible practices in everything we do, from sourcing ingredients to reducing waste. We work with local roasters to source the finest beans, and our skilled baristas take pride in creating the perfect cup for each and every customer.
