Terms & Condition

  • Orders can only be placed online through our website for Click and Collect. We do not accept orders via phone, email or any other means.
  • Click and Collect orders will be available for collection during our opening hours. Please check our website for our current opening hours.
  • Orders must be placed at least 15 minutes before the desired collection time. Orders placed less than 15 minutes before the desired collection time may not be fulfilled.
  • If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. Changes or cancellations may not be possible if your order has already been prepared.
  • We will do our best to have your order ready for collection at the specified time. However, we cannot guarantee that your order will be ready at the exact time specified due to unforeseen circumstances, such as high demand or technical issues.
  • All Click and Collect orders are subject to availability. If we are unable to fulfill your order for any reason, we will contact you as soon as possible to arrange a refund.
